Class MapStore<T>

A reactive map store. [UNSTABLE: DO NOT USE!] Implements set(key, value), remove(key), clear(), transform(key, fn), and get(key). set() sends a "set" event, remove() sends a "remove" event, clear() sends a "clear" event, and transform() sends a "mutation" event.
